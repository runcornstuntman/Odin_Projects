Japanese Breakfast Rice

Is buttered toast getting a little boring? Mix up your mornings with this tasty dish!

If you are interested in making a simple breakfast that could be premade for those rushed mornings, than look no further!

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 Teaspoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Cup cold cooked rice, or more (to taste)
  • 1 egg or more (to taste)
  • 2 green onions, sliced, or more (to taste)
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce, or to taste

Directions

  1. Break the eggs in a microwave-proof mixing bowl. Add in the milk and salt, and then mix well.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Spread the rice in a flat layer in hot oil; cook until crispy on the bottom, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Stir the egg into the rice until scrambled, about 2 minutes. Sprinkle the green onions on top and season with soy sauce.
