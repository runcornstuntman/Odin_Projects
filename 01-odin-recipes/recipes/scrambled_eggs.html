Microwave Scrambled Eggs

So you want to make scrambled eggs but only have minutes?

Fortunately, with this recipe you will learn how to use a microwave in order to cook consistantly fluffy scrambled eggs. If this sounds like what is missing from your morning meals, please read on!

Ingredients Needed

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup of your finest Milk
  • 1/8 Teaspoon o' Salt

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients listed above.
  2. Break the eggs in a microwave-proof mixing bowl. Add in the milk and salt, and then mix well.
  3. Put the bowl into the microwave and cook on high power for 30 seconds. Remove the bowl, beat the eggs very well, scraping down the sides of the bowl, and then return to the microwave for another 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat step 3, stirring every 30 seconds for up to 2 and a half minutes. Stop when the eggs have the consistency you desire.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!

Yummy twists you could add to change the dish up!

Try mixing in any of the following ingredients if you wish to add more flavour to your scrambled eggs!

  • Cheese
  • Ham or Bacon
  • Salmon
  • Veggies
  • Herbs
